What you are and aren't buying

Shadow and Advisory tiers are non-executing. The platform logs and instructs; it does not actuate a single signal. There is no code path that changes signal timing without a human turning the keys.

Verified actuation is roadmap. A read-only NTCIP 1202 controller bridge is the credible first step; a live-execution path does not exist in the pilot and we do not price one.

Certificates are issuer-verifiable (symmetric HMAC). Third parties can confirm integrity and chain membership through the Verification API; asymmetric public-key signatures are a documented roadmap item.

No deployed customers yet. The live Seattle instance is a running reference demo — not a paying municipal deployment. We will not imply otherwise.
